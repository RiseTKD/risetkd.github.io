Classes and Schools

Our Classes

We offer two different kinds classes at Rise Taekwon-Do, depending on the training experience you're looking for.

Family Classes

Looking for a more laid-back class, appropriate for families including children? That's what the family classes are for!

Family classes welcome people of all ages, including children aged 8 and up. You'll get your weekly martial arts workout to build your fitness, and will progress your Taekwon-Do skills, while training at a comfortable pace and enjoying yourself as you do it.

  • 1 hour

  • £9.50 per lesson

Advanced Classes

Serious about training in martial arts? These are the lessons for you.

Advanced classes are open to students of any grade (including beginners), aged 13 or over. Students in the advanced class will share the initial hour with family classes, but stay for an extra half hour afterward. Here we'll train harder and on more challenging techniques, in order to make the most of our weekly lesson!

  • 1h 30m

  • £12 per lesson
